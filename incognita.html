Our Antarctic World tells the story of this awesome and fragile region through natural and human history, accounts of current scientific research, and artistic responses to the seventh continent. The project is under contract with Routledge, and has been supported by a postdoctoral Environmental Fellowship through Harvard’s Center for the Environment, by the American Association of University Women, and by a Polar Archives Research Award from the Byrd Polar and Climate Research Center at the Ohio State University.
